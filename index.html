Navigate by unfolding and collapsing

By unfolding you dig deeper. You can unfold as many Chapters, Sections and Sub Sections as you like. When collapsing the parent the Configurator will remember the state of folding for its children. In case you get lost, collapse and scroll up to reach the top level, from where you can dig down again.

Color Code

The 1. Chapter (Orange) is the Wizard. With a few clicks you determine the broad strokes of your configuration. Chapter 2 ... 5 are the Parts provided by us (Blue). Chapter 6 ... 7 (Cyan) contain the parts provided by Third Parties, such as Amazon / Newegg or your favorite shop for PC parts.

Check Out

Pressing the Checkout Button opens the Checkout Window, which list all items. When clicking the item a new tab opens with its online shop page. Within the "Checkout Village Parts" window all links will open our parts on Indiegogo. Within the "Checkout Third Party" Window all links will open the respective 3rd Party Parts on Amazon or Newegg. We have no association with either shop. You are welcome to buy the same or similar part in another shop of your preference.

Help Indiegogo Checkout

What is an Indiegogo Checkout?

Indiegogo is the leading world wide fundraising site. In our case it helps us to raise the funds to put ViCase and ViDock into production. You configured your ViCase / ViDock. In this Indiegogo Checkout window you will click the links to the corresponding perks on the corresponding Indiegogo Campaign to back the perk.

Why put ViCase & ViDock on Indiegogo?

Indiegogo with its community helps ViCase and ViDock to gain popularity. In turn more people back the campaign. In turn our production benefit from economy of scale. Larger production leads to lower production costs, which we pass on to you. In turn you enjoy much lower pricing for our products on Indiegogo.

How to check out ViCase

You see three options of Perks for your ViCase in the column "Perk Discount Option". Each with a link to the corresponding Perk on the ViCase Campaign. Click on link to the Perk of your choice. The Perk will open in a new Tab. Now you are in the Indiegogo Perk Check out. Simply follow its instructions.

How to check out ViCase Extras

In case you selected ViCase Extras, repeat the same procedure. To simplify your life we listed all items in the table "ViCase Extra Perk and we computed the Total Cost of all ViCase Extra Items. You can pay for them clicking on the link "ViCase Extra Perk". Enter the Total Cost of ViCase Extra into the "Gift" field of that perk and follow the Indiegogo procedure.
